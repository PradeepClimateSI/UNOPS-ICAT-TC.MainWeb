About the TC toolkit

The ICAT Transformational Change Assessment Tool is a digital soloution that aids users in evaluating the potential or realized effects of policies and actions (interventions) on Transformational Change.

There are three main tools in TC Toolkit




Carbon Market Tool

Assess Transformational Change impact of the Carbon Market interventions




Investor & Private Sector Tool

Assess Transformational Change impact of the investment interventions


Other Intervention Tool

Assess Transformational Change impact of the other types of interventions

Get started with TC Toolkit

ICAT TC Toolkit is a free software.If you are interested in using TC Toolkit and wish to learn more about the tool and its potential application, please contact the ICAT Secretariat: icat@unops.org
