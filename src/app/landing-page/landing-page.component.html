About the Transformational Change Toolkit

The ICAT Transformational Change Toolkit helps ensure that interventions (policies, projects, or actions) have a fundamental and lasting positive impact on climate mitigation and adaptation as well as the sustainable development goals. The toolkit is based on the .

The toolkit includes the following tools:

Carbon market tool

Assess the transformational impact of any intervention that has or may have a carbon market component, i.e. all or part of the emission reductions or removals will be traded.

Investment tool

Assess the transformational impact of any intervention that is financed (at least in part) by sources other than public budgets.

General tool

Assess the transformational impact of any other intervention, for example, public policies.

Portfolio tool

Assess the aggregated impacts of grouped interventions, including commitments like nationally determined contributions.

Get started with the toolkit

The ICAT Transformational Change Toolkit is a free to use software. If you are interested in using the toolkit and wish to learn more about its potential application, please contact the ICAT Secretariat: icat@unops.org

Why do we need to calculate transformational change?

To help users assess the extent of transformation expected or achieved by policies

To help decision makers develop effective strategies for transformational change

To support transparent and consistent monitoring and reporting of transformational impacts
