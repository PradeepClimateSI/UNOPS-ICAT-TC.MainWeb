About the TC toolkit

The ICAT Transformational Change Assessment Tool is a digital solution that aids users in evaluating the potential or realized effects of policies and actions (Interventions) on Transformational Change.

Why do we need to calculate Transformational Change?

To help users assess the extent of transformation expected or achieved by policies

To help decision makers develop effective strategies for transformational change

To support transparent and consistent monitoring and reporting of transformational impacts

The Toolkit includes a set of tools, which operationalises and adapts the ICAT Transformational Change Methodology

Carbon Market Tool

To assess the transformational impact of interventions with a carbon market component

Investor & Private Sector Tool

To assess the transformational impact of investments by funds or private companies

Other Intervention Tool

To the other tools and is intended to assess the transformational impact of all those interventions that do not fall under any of the two other tools

Portfolio Tool

To assess the aggregation and comparison of the transformational impacts of several single interventions grouped under a portfolio of interventions
